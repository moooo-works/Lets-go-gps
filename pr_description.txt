Implementation Details:
- **Notification Channel ID:** `MockLocationServiceChannel`
- **startForegroundService / startForeground:** `startForegroundService` is called from `MapViewModel` via `ContextCompat.startForegroundService`. `startForeground` is called immediately in `MockLocationService.onStartCommand` to ensure it executes within the OS time limit.
- **Stop Flow:** The `handleStop()` method in `MockLocationService` explicitly calls `stopLocationPushJob()` (cancels the coroutine job), `routeSimulator.stop()`, and `mockEngine.teardownMockProvider()` before calling `stopForeground(true)` and `stopSelf()`. This guarantees the route job stops and the provider is torn down.
